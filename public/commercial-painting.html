Commercial Painting FAQs

Can you work around our business hours? ❯

Absolutely. We specialize in flexible scheduling, including evenings, weekends, and phased approaches, specifically to minimize disruption to your employees, customers, and operations. Let us know your requirements, and we'll create a plan that works.

What types of commercial properties do you paint? ❯

We paint a wide range of commercial and institutional properties, including: offices, retail storefronts, restaurants, warehouses, medical/dental facilities, churches, nursing homes, schools, light industrial buildings, apartment complexes (common areas), and HOA properties.

Are you fully licensed and insured for commercial work? ❯

Yes. This is crucial for commercial work. Dependable Painting carries comprehensive General Liability insurance ($1 Million per occurrence / $2 Million aggregate) and Workers' Compensation insurance. We prioritize safety and compliance and readily provide Certificates of Insurance (COIs) to our commercial clients upon request.

What paints do you use for high-traffic commercial areas? ❯

We utilize durable, high-performance commercial-grade paints from Sherwin-Williams, often selecting low-VOC or zero-VOC options for occupied spaces. For demanding environments like corridors, break rooms, or warehouses, we recommend and use products specifically designed for longevity and easy maintenance, such as scuff-resistant paints (like Pro Industrial High Performance Coatings or Scuff-Tuff®), epoxy coatings, or urethane enamels.

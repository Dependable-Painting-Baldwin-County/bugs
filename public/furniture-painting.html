Don't replace weathered patio furniture – restore it! Dependable Painting professionally cleans, preps, and paints or stains your outdoor furniture for a fresh look that withstands the Alabama elements.

Breathing New Life into Your Patio Set

Types of Furniture We Refinish

We handle various materials common in outdoor furniture:

  • Wood: Teak, Cedar, Pine, Eucalyptus, etc. (Staining or Painting)
  • Metal: Wrought Iron, Cast Aluminum, Steel (Rust treatment & Painting)
  • Plastic/Resin: Requires specialized prep and paint for adhesion.
  • Wicker (Natural & Synthetic): Careful cleaning and often spray application.

Our Refinishing Process

Tailored preparation for each material:

  • Deep Cleaning: Removing dirt, pollen, mildew, algae, and oxidation.
  • Surface Prep: Sanding wood, scuffing plastic/metal, wire brushing rust.
  • Priming: Applying appropriate primers (rust-inhibitive for metal, bonding for plastic, stain-blocking for wood).
  • Finish Coat: Applying durable exterior-grade paints (like SW Emerald Urethane) or stains using brush, roller, or sprayer for a smooth finish.

Benefits of Refinishing

Why restore instead of replace?

  • Cost-Effective: Significantly cheaper than buying new, high-quality furniture.
  • Eco-Friendly: Keeps usable items out of landfills.
  • Customization: Update colors to match your current outdoor decor.
  • Protection: Renews protection against sun, rain, and humidity, extending furniture life.

Outdoor Furniture Painting FAQs

What types of outdoor furniture can you paint? ❯

We can refinish most common outdoor furniture materials, including wood (like teak, cedar, pine), metal (wrought iron, cast aluminum, steel), and many types of plastic or resin chairs and tables. The preparation process varies significantly based on the material to ensure proper adhesion and durability.

Is it worth painting old patio furniture? ❯

Often, yes! High-quality outdoor furniture can be quite expensive to replace. If the structure is still sound, professional refinishing can dramatically restore its appearance and protect it from further weathering for significantly less than the cost of buying new pieces.

What kind of paint lasts best on outdoor furniture? ❯

We use premium exterior-grade paints specifically designed for the furniture material. For wood and metal, products like Sherwin-Williams Emerald® Urethane Trim Enamel offer excellent durability, adhesion, UV protection, and flexibility needed for outdoor exposure. For plastics, specialized bonding primers and compatible topcoats are essential.

How do you prepare furniture for painting? ❯

Thorough preparation is critical for a lasting finish. This always includes deep cleaning to remove dirt, pollen, mildew, and oxidation. Wood is typically sanded. Metal requires rust removal and treatment. Plastics and glossy surfaces need scuff sanding and often a special bonding primer. The exact steps depend entirely on the material and its condition.

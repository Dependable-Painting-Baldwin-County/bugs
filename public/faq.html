Frequently Asked Questions

General Business

What deposit is required? ❯

We collect a 30% deposit on project start day. No upfront booking fee. Final payment only after walkthrough.

Are you licensed and insured? ❯

Yes, fully licensed & insured in Alabama (general liability + workers comp). Documentation available upon request.

What areas do you serve? ❯

Fairhope, Daphne, Spanish Fort, Bay Minette, Mobile and surrounding Baldwin & Mobile County communities.

How do estimates work? ❯

We offer free same-day or next-day estimates. After scope review you receive a written proposal detailing prep, products, timeline and price.

Interior Painting

Which paint lines can I choose? ❯

Sherwin-Williams SuperPaint (value), Duration (durability), Emerald (premium). We match sheen and product to usage (e.g. Emerald on kitchens/baths for scrubbability).

Do I need to move furniture? ❯

Please remove fragile/personal items. We handle large furniture, cover floors/fixtures, and establish clean staging areas.

How long does a typical room take? ❯

Most 12'x12' rooms: 1–2 days including repairs, priming, two finish coats, detail touch-ups and cleanup.

Are fumes an issue? ❯

We use low or zero VOC paints and promote ventilation. Odor dissipates quickly so spaces are usable soon after drying.

Exterior Painting

How do you prepare exterior surfaces? ❯

Wash, scrape failing paint, sand feather edges, caulk gaps, prime bare & tannin-prone wood, spot repair rot before finish coats.

What about weather delays? ❯

We monitor humidity, temperature & rain windows; adjust schedule to ensure proper curing and coating performance.

Which exterior paint lines do you use? ❯

Duration & Emerald for superior film integrity and mildew resistance; specialty elastomeric or stain systems where required.

Cabinet Painting

What is your cabinet process? ❯

Label & remove doors, clean/degrease, sand + vacuum, prime with bonding primer, spray multiple enamel coats, cure, reassemble with new hardware (optional).

Will the finish last? ❯

Yes. Proper prep + high-quality enamel yields a hard, washable surface resistant to normal kitchen wear.

Can I stay in the home during cabinet work? ❯

Absolutely. We create contained spray areas & ventilation setups minimizing disruption and odor.

Commercial Painting

How do you minimize business disruption? ❯

Flexible scheduling (evenings/weekends), sectioned work zones, and clear daily communication to keep operations running.

Do you handle safety & compliance? ❯

We follow OSHA safe practices, carry insurance, and can coordinate with facilities for access, security & permitting needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get an estimate? ❯

Simply fill out our contact form or give us a call at (251) 423-5855. We offer free, no-obligation estimates, often same-day or next-day, after discussing or viewing your project.

What areas do you serve? ❯

We proudly serve Baldwin County and Mobile County in Alabama, including Fairhope, Daphne, Spanish Fort, Mobile, Bay Minette, and surrounding communities.

What kind of paint do you use? ❯

We primarily use high-quality Sherwin-Williams paints (like Duration®, Emerald®, SuperPaint®) suited for the specific job, ensuring durability and a great finish. We can discuss specific product lines during your estimate.

Are you licensed and insured? ❯

Yes, Dependable Painting is fully licensed and insured (General Liability & Workers' Compensation) for your peace of mind. We can provide documentation upon request.

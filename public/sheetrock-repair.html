Drywall & Finishing FAQs

How messy is popcorn ceiling removal? ❯

It can be messy, but we take extensive precautions. We cover floors, walls, and furniture completely with plastic sheeting and use dust control measures to minimize spread, followed by thorough cleanup.

Can you match my existing wall texture? ❯

Yes, matching existing textures is a key part of our repair process. We can replicate common textures like orange peel, knockdown, or skip trowel to ensure seamless repairs.

What if removing wallpaper damages the drywall? ❯

It's common for wallpaper removal to reveal or cause minor drywall damage. Our service includes necessary repairs like skim coating or patching to ensure a smooth surface before priming and painting.

How long do drywall repairs take? ❯

Small repairs (nail pops, minor cracks) can often be done in a few hours. Larger repairs or texture work may require multiple visits over 1-3 days to allow for drying time between compound coats and before priming/painting.

Do you prime and paint after repairs? ❯

Absolutely. Priming repaired areas is essential for an even paint finish. We offer complete painting services to finish the job seamlessly after repairs are complete and fully cured.

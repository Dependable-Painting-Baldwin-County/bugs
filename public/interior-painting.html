Interior Painting FAQs

Do you repair small damage before painting?

Yes. We fix nail pops, small holes, and surface dings as part of prep. For larger issues, see Drywall & Texture Repair.

Which paint should I choose?

SuperPaint is great value, Duration boosts durability & mildew resistance, and Emerald provides top-tier finish & washability. We’ll help you match the product to the space.

How do you protect my home?

We protect floors and furnishings, isolate dust, and perform daily cleanup with walkthroughs so you’re always in the loop.
